Common Interview Questions:

Common Interview Questions include:
Tell me a little about yourself.
What are your biggest weaknesses?
What are your biggest strengths?
Where do you see yourself in five years?
Out of all the other candidates, why should we hire you?
How did you learn about the opening?
Why do you want this job?
What do you consider to be your biggest professional achievement?
Tell me about the last time a co-worker or customer got angry with you. What happened?
Describe your dream job.
Why do you want to leave your current job?
What kind of work environment do you like best?
Resume Tips:

Identify Accomplishments not Just Job Descriptions.
Quantify Your Accomplishments.
"Cater Your Resume for your subject on interest.
Replace your Objective" with a "Career Summary
Network. Network. Network.
NO large paragraphs (1-2 sentences maximum)
Keep your points flowing
Any references you cite should be included on a different document
Organzie content of bulletpoints based on category (general, education, work experience, Extracurricular activities)
